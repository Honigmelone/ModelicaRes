.. only:: latex

   This is the user's guide of utilities for the FCSys_ package.  These Python_
   modules may be used to analyze and plot results from Modelica_ simulations.
   There are also specific methods to handle results of fuel cell simulations.
   The methods rely on scipy_, matplotlib_, and python-control_.  The following
   chapters each describe a module of the FCSys utilities.

   .. Seealso:: The buildingspy_
      (http://simulationresearch.lbl.gov/modelica/buildingspy/) and DyMat_
      (http://www.j-raedler.de/projects/dymat/) projects provide other
      Python_ modules that are related.
